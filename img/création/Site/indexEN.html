PRESENTATION

We are a non-profit event association founded by alumni prisoners in search of smiles and joy. We like to play with the limits and mainly the limits of your imagination. Indeed, our intention is to organize completely unforeseeable events. You must let yourself be tempted by the unknown, the risk and without fear of having fun! Expect the unexpected because we offer you surprise services.
